About us

Highland Capital focuses on providing growth capital and support to businesses in Kyrgyzstan. We provide equity and mezzanine investments to SMEs to help them institutionalize and grow into large regional champions.

Our team is run by a team of seasoned investment professionals that combine best western practice with intimate knowledge. We started our operations in Bishkek in June 2014 and have a successfully history of sourcing, evaluating, and executing investments in Kyrgyzstan.

The Fund’s investment strategy focuses on two major themes:

Domestic demand and consumer- oriented sectors

Kyrgyzstan’s growing population and burgeoning middle class, along with its remittances-driven economy, make the consumer sectors a strong bet:

  • Food & beverage
  • Residential and commercial real estate
  • Healthcare
  • Telecom
  • Education
Export driven sectors

Kyrgyzstan is a member of the Eurasian Economic Union, a 186 million person market, and has strong trade relationships with both China and Russia:

  • Organic agriculture
  • Food & beverage: Dairy and meat processing
  • Tourism
  • Software & technology development; outsourcing services

About Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyz Republic is located at the heart of Eurasia benefitting from a border with China (#1 trading partner and #2 foreign investor) and a customs union with Russia.

Kyrgyzstan experinced remarkable economic growth over the past two decades. Between 2007 and 2017, Kyrgyzstan’s compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of GDP was 6.5%.
