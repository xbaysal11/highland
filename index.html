About us

Highland Capital focuses on providing growth capital and support to businesses in Kyrgyzstan. We provide equity and mezzanine investments to SMEs to help them institutionalize and grow into large regional champions.

Our team is run by a team of seasoned investment professionals that combine best western practice with intimate knowledge. We started our operations in Bishkek in June 2014 and have a successfully history of sourcing, evaluating, and executing investments in Kyrgyzstan.
